GLOBAL UI STRUCTURE
================================================================================

The app uses a card-based, single-page layout with screen switching.
All screens share:
- Dark theme (bg: #1c1c1e, cards: #2c2c2e)
- Accent color: #007AFF (iOS blue)
- Success: #30D158 (green), Error: #FF453A (red), Warning: #FF9F0A (orange)
- Rounded corners on everything (12-16px radius)
- Smooth fade-in animations when switching screens
- Bilingual support (FR/EN toggle in top-right corner)
